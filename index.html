No Longer Human


Osamu Dazai
In the novel "No Longer Human", Dazai uses this book to describe his personal unattached view at the society he lives in. Dazai, being born in a aristocratic family while being influenced by western culture, felt like he wasn't human compared to that of his faimily or people around him in his school.
This novel is highly reccomended to those who feel like they might be detached from society, or people interested in the struggle of someone who grew up differently. I would give this novel a solid 4.5/5 due to it's complex and diverse vocabulary and amazing narrative all the way through.
